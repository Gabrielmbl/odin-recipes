Chocolate Chips Cookies

Description

This chocolate chip cookie recipe is adored by most for its soft, chewy texture and balanced flavor. Made with butter, sugars, eggs, vanilla, baking soda, salt, flour, chocolate chips, and optional walnuts, these cookies are simple to make. Just mix the ingredients, drop spoonfuls onto baking sheets, bake until golden, and enjoy!

Ingredients

  • Butter
  • Eggs
  • Vanilla
  • Baking soda
  • Water
  • Salt
  • Flour
  • Chocolate chips
  • Nuts (optional)

Steps

  1. Beat the butter and sugars, then beat in the eggs and vanilla.
  2. Dissolve the baking soda in hot water and add to the mixture.
  3. Stir in the flour, chocolate chips, and walnuts.
  4. Drop dough onto a prepared baking sheet. soda
  5. Bake until the edges are golden brown.

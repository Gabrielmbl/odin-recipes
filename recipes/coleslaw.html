Coleslaw

Description

This coleslaw recipe is quick and easy to prepare. Simply combine a bag of coleslaw mix with diced onion in a large bowl. In a separate bowl, whisk together creamy salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt until well blended. Pour the dressing over the coleslaw mixture and toss to coat. Chill the coleslaw for at least 2 hours before serving, allowing the flavors to meld together for a delicious side dish.

Ingredients

  • 1 (16 ounce) bag coleslaw mix
  • 2 tablespoons diced onion
  • ⅔ cup creamy salad dressing (such as Miracle Whip™)
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • 3 tablespoons vegetable oil
  • 1 tablespoon white vinegar
  • ½ teaspoon poppy seeds
  • ¼ teaspoon salt

Steps

  1. Combine coleslaw mix and onion in a large bowl.
  2. Whisk salad dressing, sugar, vegetable oil, vinegar, poppy seeds, and salt together in a medium bowl until blended. Pour dressing over coleslaw mixture and toss to coat.
  3. Chill for at least 2 hours before serving.
